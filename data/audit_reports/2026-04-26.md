## Recommendations for Analyst
1. Implement a secondary-creator AST series-role audit: before accepting H2H records at T2 for non-primary creators (raw_avgs AST < 4.0), check the current series diary for evidence that a teammate has assumed facilitation duties. Barrett's H2H 5/5 was voided by Barnes becoming TOR's primary facilitator mid-series; Ingram's H2H 6/6 was voided by the same dynamic. Add a rule: if the series diary or game log shows a teammate generating materially more AST than their season average in the last 2 games, apply an additional -5pp to secondary creator AST picks regardless of H2H record.
2. Apply a voluntary FG_COLD step-down when FG_COLD is between -12% and -14% AND opp_defense is simultaneously flagged as 'tough' or 'tightening' (def_recency delta negative): the current rule requires -15% for a mandatory step, but Sengun REB T8 missed in exactly this scenario (FG_COLD -14%, LAL def_recency -12.2%). The combined signal — near-threshold cold shooting against a tightening defense — should trigger a voluntary step-down one tier as a risk management rule, even without crossing the -15% mandatory threshold.
3. Strengthen blowout probability pricing for AST and low-tier scoring props on bench/secondary players in games where both a confirmed system-wide suppressor is active (PHI) and the opponent is at full strength: the Edgecombe PTS/AST misses both occurred in the PHI-suppressor environment with BOS fully healthy. The pre-game spread of 7.5 understated the blowout risk in this specific structural combination. Add a rule: when the picking team is a confirmed system-wide suppressor (PHI, HOU, LAL) AND faces an opponent at full strength, apply an additional -5pp to secondary scorer PTS and AST picks regardless of spread_abs — the suppressor mechanism implies game-script risk beyond what spread alone captures.
4. For bench players with average minutes between 20-26 in blowout-likely scenarios on the winning side (Harper AST T2, projected in a series where SAS had won G3 by 12 on the road), apply a blowout game-script AST discount of -5pp even when the pre-game spread is below the formal threshold. The series diary context (SAS leading 3-0, prior road win by 12) should feed a blowout probability estimate that overrides the raw spread_abs threshold; specifically, when the series diary shows the picking team won their most recent road game by 10+, treat the current road game as having elevated blowout probability and reduce confidence on bench-player AST props accordingly.
5. Recalibrate the 70-75% confidence band: actual hit rate 69.2% is 3.3pp below expected 72.5%, and all 4 AST misses (Barrett, Harper, Ingram, Edgecombe) plus the Sengun REB miss fell in this band. The 70-75% band is systematically producing results below its stated probability, suggesting these are marginal picks that should either be stepped down further or skipped. Implement a rule: no AST pick below 76% confidence in a playoff blowout-risk game (spread_abs > 6 or series diary showing prior blowout), and treat 70-72% AST picks as skip candidates unless iron_floor is confirmed and series data is 3/3 at the selected tier.
