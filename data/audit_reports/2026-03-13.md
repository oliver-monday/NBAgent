## Recommendations for Analyst
1. Implement a mandatory same-day final lineup confirmation step (within 60-90 minutes of tip-off) for any player whose injury_status_at_check is NOT_LISTED but who has missed or been limited in any of the prior 3 games. Sengun's dual-miss DNP (0 PTS, 0 AST, 0 min) was entirely preventable with a later availability check.
2. For 3PM picks on the winning side of games with spread_abs ≥ 12, apply an additional -5pp deduction beyond the standard BLOWOUT -10% (total -15pp from base), or hard-cap 3PM confidence at 70% in these scenarios. Mitchell's 3PM miss shows that shooting volume compresses faster than PTS/AST in blowout wins due to early benching and reduced fourth-quarter minutes.
3. When the MIN AST suppressor watch item is active AND the opponent also carries a tough AST DvP rating, apply a minimum -10pp adjustment to ALL AST tiers (including T2) for non-primary ball-handlers. Randle's T2 AST miss (1 actual vs. 2 pick) was the product of two compounding suppressor signals that the system treated as independent — they are not.
4. Alperen Sengun's two picks (PTS and AST) were included in two separate parlays ('Iron Floor Trio' and 'Scottie-Mitchell Cross-Game Lock'), causing both parlays to miss on a single DNP event. Enforce a rule: no single player may appear in more than one parlay on the same day. Player concentration across parlays converts a single availability event into a multi-parlay loss.
5. The 3PM step-down rule (trend=down → mandatory T2→T1 step) combined with iron_floor did not prevent Mitchell's 3PM miss. Consider adding a hard skip rule for 3PM when both trend=down AND blowout_risk=True are simultaneously present, regardless of iron_floor status — the iron_floor tag on 3PM appears to provide less protection than on PTS/AST in compressed blowout minutes.
