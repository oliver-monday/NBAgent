2. Revise the iron_floor 3PM blowout override: currently, iron_floor overrides the 80% non-iron-floor cap and the blowout_secondary_scorer skip does not apply to 3PM. Add a rule: in playoff games where spread_abs > 10 AND the player is a secondary scorer (raw_avgs PTS < 22), apply an additional -5% blowout penalty to iron_floor 3PM picks regardless of iron_floor status. This directly addresses the Vassell T1 miss — 34 minutes in a blowout win but 0-for on threes as garbage time compressed catch-and-shoot attempts.
3. Strengthen the AST competitive_split gate: if a player's competitive_split for AST at any tier is < 70%, that tier should be flagged as hard-skip regardless of the blowout_games rate. The Harper AST T2 miss was foreshadowed by competitive=68% in the tier_walk — the analyst noted it but overrode it using blowout context. Add an explicit rule: competitive_split < 70% on AST = skip the tier, full stop. Do not allow blowout_games rate to rescue an AST pick when the competitive split is below floor.
4. For REB picks where a player's series avg is more than 3x their regular-season avg (e.g., Vassell: 7.0 series vs. 2.4 season), apply a series-inflation warning that caps confidence at 72% regardless of H2H or series hit rate, and require the pick_value to be <= the player's regular-season avg + 1.5. Vassell REB T4 (pick_value=4) with a 2.4 season avg and 1 actual result in 34 blowout minutes confirms that series-inflated REB averages do not transfer to blowout garbage-time contexts.
5. Implement a min_floor / pick_value ratio gate for AST props: if a player's avg_minutes - min_floor < 6 (meaning realistic low-minutes games are within 6 minutes of the floor), AND pick_value >= raw_avgs AST - 0.5 (thin margin), the pick should be stepped down one tier or skipped. This gate would have caught the Harper miss and prevented it from running.
