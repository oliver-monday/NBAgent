2. When BOS rotation uncertainty is flagged (Tatum return, managed minutes), do not pick any BOS secondary scorer PTS prop above T8 in the same session — Derrick White's dual miss (PTS + 3PM) despite 36 minutes confirms that CLE's elite switching suppression compounds the rotation uncertainty risk in a way that makes secondary BOS PTS/3PM props unreliable anchor picks.
3. Add a 'skip' trigger for assist props when the opponent is specifically profiled as suppressing opponent assist rates AND the pick is at the minimum qualifying hit rate (7/10) — Luka AST T6 vs NYK met both suppression criteria, and marginal-threshold AST picks against low-pace assist-suppressing teams should require 8+/10 to qualify.
4. For BLOWOUT_RISK games where the subject player's team is the large underdog (spread +8 or worse), apply a mandatory skip to any PTS pick on the losing team's secondary scorer (non-primary option) regardless of hit rate — Ingram's 64% blowout-game drop was a known signal that was acknowledged but not acted on; secondary scorers on trailing teams face asymmetric usage compression late in blowouts.
5. Cross-reference REB picks for bigs playing against MIA with their low-pace zone-defense profile before selecting — Duren's miss at 5 (1 below T6) in a 26-minute game shows that MIA's rebounding suppression + zone scheme is a specific model gap for rim-running centers, and T6+ REB picks for non-elite rebounders against MIA should require a 25th-percentile L10 floor of at least 7 (not exactly 7) to clear the gate.
