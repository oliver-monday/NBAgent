## Recommendations for Analyst
1. 3PM HARD AVOID directive enforcement: When a per-player dossier flags 'HARD AVOID 3PM' for a specific player in playoff context (e.g. Mitchell H27: 43% actual vs 76% assigned, -7.5pp suppressor), treat this as a near-mandatory skip regardless of within-series form or H2H recency. Series 4/4 and H2H 6/6 at T2 are insufficient to override a documented playoff-specific mechanism where the player's scoring style structurally shifts away from threes. Specifically: when HARD AVOID 3PM is annotated AND the player's series scoring pattern is consistent with drive/FT dominance (e.g. Mitchell's 43-point game with 1 three), the 3PM pick should require iron_floor confirmation — not just 'not overridden' — to proceed. In the absence of a confirmed iron_floor after VOLATILE override, default to SKIP when H27 or equivalent dossier flags HARD AVOID.
2. Compounding advisory stacking for 3PM: Introduce a compounding rule for 3PM picks: if two or more of the following are present simultaneously — (a) VOLATILE tag, (b) iron_floor=false after override, (c) dossier HARD AVOID or strong suppressor annotation, (d) game-script context suggesting drive/FT-dominant scoring — the pick should be skipped regardless of within-series hit rate. The Mitchell 3PM pick activated all four conditions; the current rules allowed it to survive each condition individually while the combination was structurally unsound. A compound-skip threshold of 3 or more active conditions would have correctly blocked this pick.
3. CLV warning threshold enforcement review: Three picks on this slate (Mitchell AST, Mobley AST, Cunningham AST) triggered ast_lost_close CLV warnings (live_clv_pp of -1.67, -0.71, and -2.45 respectively) with proposed 5pp penalties that were all flagged as applied=false. All three hits landed comfortably above threshold. This confirms the H34 backtest_clv_ast_disagree signal is not yet reliably predictive on its own — applying a mandatory 5pp penalty for any ast_lost_close trigger would have been overcautious here. Continue not auto-applying this penalty; require validation from a larger sample before operationalizing the proposed 5pp deduction as mandatory.
4. Overtime game reconstruction for floor props: This game went to overtime (CLE 117, DET 113 — confirmed by Cunningham 48 minutes played). Multiple picks benefited from expanded floor production in OT minutes: Harden scored 30 (vs T10), Mobley had 8 AST (vs T2), Cunningham had 9 AST (vs T6) in 48 minutes. None of the pick confidence values were modeled with overtime probability factored in. For elimination games or series-deciding games where overtime probability is elevated (e.g. spread ≤5, series tied or 3-2/2-3), consider a modest +2pp floor-pick upward adjustment to reflect the additional possession volume that overtime provides for counting stat props. This is particularly relevant for AST and REB floor picks at minimum tiers.
