## Recommendations for Analyst
1. Implement a blowout-road-underdog PTS cap: when a player's team is (a) on the road, (b) down 2-0 or 0-2 in a playoff series (desperate context for the HOME team, not the road team), and (c) the road team is a confirmed underdog by ≥4 points, apply a mandatory -5pp confidence penalty to all PTS picks for the road team's primary scorers and do not select T20+ PTS picks without iron_floor. This would have prevented or flagged the Mitchell PTS T20 miss.
2. Add a lineup_update veto rule for AST props: when the afternoon lineup agent issues a direction=down amendment that revises AST confidence below the prop-type floor (68%), automatically void the AST pick rather than running it at the floor. The Jokic AST T8 pick ran at 70% despite the amendment dropping it to 67% — this is an execution gap. The amendment agent is a downstream check; its below-floor revisions must trigger auto-void.
3. For non-iron-floor 3PM T1 picks: require raw_avg_3PM ≥ 2.0 OR iron_floor = true before selection. Players with raw_avg_3PM between 1.0–1.9 and no iron_floor should be skipped at T1 regardless of overall hit rate, because a single 0-for-2 or 0-for-3 shooting night (entirely within normal variance for a 1.4 avg player) collapses the prop. Mikal Bridges (1.4 raw avg, VOLATILE, 21 minutes) is the prototype case.
4. Do not boost PTS confidence for absence-driven usage redistribution when pre-game signals include FG_COLD or VOLATILE and the opponent is a top-6 defensive team. The Jamal Murray amendment flagged 'upside revised to 85%' due to Jokic OUT, but Murray's FG_COLD:-9% + VOLATILE + DEN losing by 17 produced a 16-point output. Absence-driven upside revisions should be capped at +5pp maximum for VOLATILE PTS props and should not override FG_COLD signals already present at pick time.
5. Flag hero-scorer AST compression risk for non-primary playmakers (raw_avg_AST < 5.0) in close-game contexts: when a player has a PTS/AST ratio > 5:1 in their recent L5 games AND the game spread is < 4 points (close game likely), apply a mandatory -5pp penalty to their AST props and do not select AST T2+ without iron_floor. CJ McCollum (23 PTS, 1 AST in a 1-point game) and Jalen Brunson (26 PTS, 4 AST in a 1-point game) both exhibited this pattern on the same slate — two independent data points suggesting the signal is real.
