However, for future sessions: when TOR (or any team) is playing a road game against a team with top-10 defensive rating AND the player has trend=down on the prop type, treat the combination as equivalent to a known BLOWOUT_RISK scenario and apply the -10% blowout penalty preemptively. 5. The 70-75% confidence band went 5/8 (62.5%) against an expected 72.5%, underperforming by 10 percentage points. All three misses occurred in this band. Review whether picks in this band with two or more compounding risk factors (VOLATILE tag, trend=down, road underdog, tough defense) should be elevated to 76%+ requirements or skipped entirely. Consider a rule: picks in the 70-75% band require at least one positive signal (iron_floor, consistent tag, or 10/10 hit rate) to qualify; picks with only baseline qualifying hit rates (7/10 at T15, 9/10 at T10) plus two risk factors should be stepped down or skipped.
