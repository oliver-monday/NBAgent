## Recommendations for Analyst
1. Institute a losing-side blowout buffer for road underdogs on PTS props near the threshold floor: when spread_abs is 4–7 (moderate underdog), apply a precautionary -5% confidence reduction to any PTS pick where pick_value is within 3 points of the T-floor (i.e., a near-threshold pick). A single short fourth quarter on the losing side produces exactly the -1 margin miss seen with Barnes today. This would have reduced confidence from 84% to ~79% or prompted a tier step-down on Barnes PTS.
2. Develop a MIN REB context flag analogous to the MIN AST suppressor. Today's KD REB miss (actual=3 vs. pick=4) in a competitive HOU @ MIN game is consistent with a pattern where MIN's frontcourt competition and scheme suppress opposing big-man rebounding. Track REB hit rates vs. MIN in the H15 backtest to evaluate whether a formal suppressor signal (similar to the AST suppressor at −29.5pp) is emerging for REB props as well.
3. For PTS top_pick designations on road underdogs, require at least one of: (a) spread_abs < 3 (near-even game), (b) iron_floor tag, or (c) pick_value at least 5 points below the player's L10 average. Barnes PTS at T10 was exactly at the tier floor with no iron_floor tag, on the road as a meaningful underdog — the top_pick flag elevated it inappropriately. Tighten this gate.
4. When a player's REB prop is targeted vs. a team with a documented defensive scheme signal (MIN switching, BOS switching, MIL switching), apply the same scrutiny logic used for AST props vs. those teams — prefer one tier lower or require a higher hit-rate threshold. The MIN AST suppressor framework is the right template; extend it to REB for known frontcourt-competition teams even before a formal backtest confirmation.
5. The 81-85% confidence band underperformed today (75.0% actual vs. 83.0% expected), driven by the Barnes PTS miss. Review all picks in this band for the pattern of 'consistent tag + 10/10 hit rate + near-threshold pick value on a road underdog' — this combination may be systematically overconfident. The 70-75% band's 11/11 performance suggests the system's conservatism at lower confidence levels is correctly calibrated; the 81-85% band may benefit from an additional gate requiring either iron_floor or pick_value ≥ 5 points below the tier threshold before emitting above 80%.
